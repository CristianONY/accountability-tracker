About & Methodology

Purpose

A transparent, daily-updated registry of corporations that financially support, enable, or profit from: the Trump administration's agenda, Israeli military operations in Gaza, ICE detention, private equity extraction, environmental destruction, labor violations, surveillance of civilians, child exploitation, bipartisan corporate capture, and disinformation campaigns.

Categories

Pro-Trump — supports/funds Trump or Republican agenda. Pro-Israel — supports Israeli military or occupation. Both — both. Bipartisan — funds both parties to protect corporate interests. Global Harm — systemic harm not tied to one party (ICE, private equity, opioids, child exploitation, labor abuse).

Impact Score

5Direct mass harm: weapons to active conflict, child exploitation, $2M+ donation, major detention infrastructure
4Significant: $1-2M donation, major defense/ICE contract, top BDS target, key legislation passed via lobbying
3Moderate: <$1M donation, lobbying win, price gouging, deregulation benefit
2Indirect: PAC support, minor lobbying, DEI rollback, peripheral ties
1Peripheral or emerging concern

Update Schedule

An AI agent with real-time web search runs daily at 7:00 AM CT across 90+ issue areas. Historical incidents are permanently logged and never deleted.
